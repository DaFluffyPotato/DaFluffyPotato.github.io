Who is DaFluffyPotato?

DaFluffyPotato(me) is a game developer who mostly works in Python and is known to make almost anything with Pygame even if it doesn't make sense. Most notably, I made an IRC client with Pygame and made the questionable decision of multithreading it. I also almost exclusively make my games with pixel art.

I have made many games, the more recent of which(although almost exclusively game jam games) are on Itch.io.



But Who is DaFluffyPotato?

DaFluffyPotato is actually one of my various usernames. I originally used CMLSC as my username for game development stuff, but now I go with DaFluffyPotato because it's more recognizable. Sometimes you might see me as LarryMcMuffin though.
