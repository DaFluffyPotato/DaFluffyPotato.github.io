Who is DaFluffyPotato?

DaFluffyPotato(me) is a game developer who mostly works in Python and is known to make almost anything with Pygame even if it doesn't make sense. Most notably, I made an IRC client with Pygame and made the questionable decision of multithreading it. I also almost exclusively make my games with pixel art.

I have made many games, the more recent of which(although almost exclusively game jam games) are on Itch.io.



But Who is DaFluffyPotato?

DaFluffyPotato is actually one of my various usernames. I originally used CMLSC as my username for game development stuff, but now I go with DaFluffyPotato because it's more recognizable. Sometimes you might see me as LarryMcMuffin though.



My Origins

I've been programming since 2013. Because I'm very resistant to change, I've been using Python and Pygame this whole time. I entered my first Ludum Dare in April of 2016(LD 35) while sick. I haven't missed a single Ludum Dare since.

The FAQ That I Have Been Asked


Where does the name DaFluffyPotato come from?

It doesn't actually mean anything. I just came up with it out of nowhere and I liked the sound of it. I used to go by CMLSC(and still do sometimes), but nobody could remember the letters, so I needed a new name anyway.


Why do your games look like FEZ?

I have been inspired by FEZ's pixel art in the past, but most of my inspiration comes from Daniel Linssen and rxi. I also pick up other techniques from just looking at pixel art or figuring it out on my own.


How did you learn to make games?

When it comes to Python and pixel art, foley, and voice acting, I'm 100% self taught(which is probably why I put most of my code in one file and don't use OOP that much). In music, which is my weak area as of now, I took 8 years of piano lessons.
